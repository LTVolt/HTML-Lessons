Buttered Noodles

Back to Main

Saucy, buttered noodles are simple to make and perfect to serve either as-is or alongside steak, chicken, or meatballs.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Boil the fettuccine in lightly salted water until it's tender, yet still firm.
  2. Drain the pasta in a colander, then return it to the pot.
  3. Mix butter, cheese, salt, and pepper into the cooked noodles until all the ingredients are evenly combined.

What to Serve With

The beauty of buttered noodles? It can work as either a side dish or an entree. What you pair with it depends on how you're serving it.
